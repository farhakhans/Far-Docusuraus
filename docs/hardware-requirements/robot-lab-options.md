# Robot Lab Options

Comprehensive hardware solutions designed for institutional deployment, supporting multiple simultaneous users and diverse robotics curricula.

## Station Configurations

### Standard Student Station
- **Quantity**: 1-4 robots per station
- **Computing**: Shared workstation with multiple robot connections
- **Safety**: Safety-rated workspace with emergency stop
- **Storage**: Component storage and tool organization
- **Documentation**: Integrated displays for instructions and diagrams

### Advanced Research Station
- **Quantity**: 1-2 advanced robots per station
- **Computing**: Dedicated high-performance workstation per robot
- **Sensors**: Extended sensor suite including 3D cameras, force sensors
- **Workspace**: Large-format workspace with modular fixtures
- **Monitoring**: Real-time performance and safety monitoring

### Demonstration Station
- **Quantity**: 1 showcase robot
- **Computing**: High-performance computing with visualization
- **Presentation**: Large display for audience viewing
- **Interaction**: Touch screen interface for visitor interaction
- **Durability**: Heavy-duty construction for frequent use

## Infrastructure Requirements

### Space Planning
- Minimum 10ft x 10ft per student station
- Climate control (temperature, humidity, dust)
- Adequate electrical service (outlets, power capacity)
- Network infrastructure (wired and wireless)
- Ventilation for 3D printing and electronics

### Safety Systems
- Emergency stop systems
- Safety barriers and interlocks
- Fire suppression systems
- First aid equipment
- Safety training materials

### Maintenance Facilities
- Electronics workbench
- 3D printing and post-processing area
- Calibration equipment
- Spare parts storage
- Documentation library
