# Welcome to Our Robotics Blog

Welcome to the official blog of the Physical AI Humanoid Robotics Education platform! This blog will serve as a resource for the latest updates, insights, and educational content related to humanoid robotics and physical AI.

<!-- truncate -->

## What to Expect {#what-to-expect}

In this blog, you'll find:

- **Educational Articles**: In-depth explanations of robotics concepts
- **Technical Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides for implementing robotic systems
- **Industry Updates**: News about the latest developments in humanoid robotics
- **Project Showcases**: Examples of student projects and achievements
- **Research Highlights**: Summaries of cutting-edge research in physical AI

## About Our Program {#about-our-program}

Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to guide you from fundamental concepts to advanced implementations in the field of humanoid robotics and physical artificial intelligence. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced roboticist, you'll find valuable content to enhance your knowledge and skills.

Stay tuned for our upcoming posts where we'll dive deeper into topics like ROS2, simulation environments, NVIDIA Isaac, and Vision-Language-Action systems.

## Connect With Us {#connect-with-us}

We encourage you to engage with our content by leaving comments, asking questions, and sharing your own experiences in robotics. Together, we can build a vibrant community of learners and practitioners in the field of humanoid robotics.
